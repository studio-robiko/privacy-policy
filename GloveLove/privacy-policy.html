Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

Summary: GloveLove does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your privacy is fully protected.

1. Introduction

GloveLove ("we," "our," or "the app") is an educational sticker application designed to promote safe sex awareness and LGBTQ+ inclusivity through expressive messaging stickers.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our approach to data handling.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any data. GloveLove operates entirely offline and does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track user behavior
  • Access device sensors or location
  • Require account creation or login
  • Connect to external servers
  • Use analytics or advertising SDKs

3. Permissions

GloveLove only requests permissions necessary for core functionality:

  • Storage: To save stickers locally on your device for sharing via messaging apps

No data is transmitted outside your device. All stickers are bundled within the app.

4. Third-Party Services

GloveLove does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

5. Messaging App Integration

GloveLove allows you to share stickers via popular messaging apps such as WhatsApp, Telegram, Signal, and others. When you share stickers, they are handled by the respective app's privacy policy. GloveLove only provides the sticker files â€” we do not access, monitor, or store your conversations.

6. Age Requirement

GloveLove is designed for users aged 16 and above due to its sexual health education content. The app is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect data from any user, regardless of age, as we do not collect any data at all.

7. Educational Purpose

GloveLove is intended as an educational tool to promote:

  • Safe sex practices and sexual health awareness
  • LGBTQ+ inclusivity and pride
  • Open, positive communication about relationships

The stickers are designed for lighthearted, expressive communication and should be used responsibly.
